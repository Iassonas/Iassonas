# 👋 Hi, I'm Iassonas Georgakopoulos

**Data Scientist & AI Engineer: NLP, RAG & Agentic Generative AI**

🇬🇷 Half Greek, 🇫🇷 half French, and fully convinced that Europe shouldn't outsource its intelligence.

I grew up between cultures, studied in Lyon, and cut my teeth on AI research in Geneva. That journey taught me something I now build around: the best technology, like the best ideas, comes from bridging worlds. Research and production, theory and impact, local roots and global ambition.

Today I design and deploy generative AI that actually **ships**: agentic LLM assistants, RAG pipelines over real business data, and the infrastructure that keeps them reliable at scale. But the bigger picture matters more to me than any single model. I want to help build a European AI ecosystem that's **sovereign, trustworthy, and ours**.

- 🔭 **Currently:** Data Scientist / AI Engineer at **Elit-Technologies** (Lyon), building agentic LLM assistants and RAG pipelines for CRM automation.
- 🧠 **Focused on:** LLM agents, retrieval-augmented generation, model evaluation, and production MLOps.
- 🛰️ **Side project:** [**docusearch.eu**](https://docusearch.eu), applications built on European AI models with EU data sovereignty at the core.
- 🤖 **Live demo:** [GPT-powered portfolio chatbot](https://iasso-gpt-chatbot-app.azurewebsites.net)
- 🌍 **Languages:** French (native) · Greek (native) · English (C2) · Spanish (B2)
- 📍 **Based in** Lyon, France. Open to roles across the Geneva / Lyon corridor.

## 🚀 What I'm Building

**🤖 Agentic CRM Assistant** *(Elit-Technologies)*
An AI agent that automates CRM workflows and customer support, with natural-language querying over thousands of tickets and docs via RAG, plus custom tools for live SQL queries and vector-store filtering. Handles ticket triage, status tracking, and EoS/EoL subscription lifecycle management. *LangChain · Qdrant · SQL · Python.*

**📚 docusearch.eu**
A side project exploring what production AI looks like when it's built on European models and EU data residency. Sovereignty as a design constraint, not an afterthought.

**🧬 Biomedical NLP Research** *(Campus Biotech, Geneva)*
End-to-end pipeline generating synthetic clinical reports, with LLMs (Llama, Mistral, Command R) fine-tuned on HPC infrastructure and a Longformer classifier built to distinguish AI-generated from expert-written reports.
